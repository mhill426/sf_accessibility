Access
for All.

Using simple principles to create more inclusive experiences.

Why

Remove barriers and rethink the interaction.

How

Focus on people and build in empathy.
“Web accessibility refers to the inclusive practice of removing barriers that prevent interaction with, or access to websites, by people with disabilities. When sites are correctly designed, developed and edited, all users have equal access to information and functionality.”
Source: Wikipedia

This is a guide on how to think, design, build, and test digital products with all of our users in mind. Though it was originally created for San Francisco, City & County departments and vendors, we realized it has the opportunity to bring awareness to an issue throughout the tech industry in which an a group of users people are not included in the process of designing, developing, or testing.

We believe our diversity in backgrounds and experiences allows us to come together to think and create products that none of us could do single-handed, so why would we release a product that any person could NOT use?
